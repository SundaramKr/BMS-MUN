About & FAQs

BMS MUN 2025 is an international Model United Nations conference hosted by BMS MUN Society. It brings together students to engage in high-quality debates, diplomatic discussions, and global problem-solving.

You can register by filling out the online form available on our website or by clickin this link. Register Now!

The individual registration fee is ₹1200 for BMSCE internals and ₹1500 for externals. The registration fee for a delegation of minimum 10 members is ₹1320 per head. This includes access to all conference sessions, delegate kits, and social events.

BMS MUN 2025 is open to high school and university students interested in diplomacy, international relations, and debating.

For any queries, you can reach out to us here or DM us on our social media pages.
